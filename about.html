About This Site !



Why did you create this website?

I created this website as part of my Harvard CS50x course. As part of this course, we must choose a "track", which are like specialisms after the first 8 weeks of learning general programmatic concepts. Out of the Games, Web and Mobile tracks, I chose Web!



What is CS50x?

CS50x is a course ran by Harvard, providing an all-round introduction to computer science, teaching students the fundamentals of computer science, how to think algorithmically and solve problems efficiently.
